Global macros show mixed bag performance. Domestic markets close in the red.This was a tough week for domestic markets with majority of indices closing in red, owing to the Israel-Hamas war, strong US dollar, FIIs' selling, Q2 results 2023 coming below estimates and rising inflation concerns. Each of the Nifty 50, Nifty Midcap 150, Nifty Small cap 250 ended on a negative note losing (-) 2.4%, (-) 2.8% and (-) 2.9% respectively.The week was also difficult for sectoral indices with all of them closing in the red. However, of all the sectors, Nifty Media, Nifty Metal and Nifty IT were at the bottom losing (-) 5.0%, (-) 3.7% and (-) 2.8% respectively.
