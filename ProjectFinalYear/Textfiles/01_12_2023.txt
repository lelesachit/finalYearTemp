Global macros a mixed bag. Domestic markets resilient.Most Indian indices closed on a positive note this week with each of the Nifty 50, Nifty Midcap 150, Nifty Small cap 250 closing in green, gaining 2.4%, 2.8% and 2.6% respectively. Nifty 50 hit a record high this week owing to faster than expected economic growth.This was also a positive week for all sectoral indices. While each of the sectoral indices closed in green, Nifty Oil and Gas, Nifty Financial Services and Nifty Media occupied the top position, gaining 4.4%, 3.6% and 3.2% respectively.
