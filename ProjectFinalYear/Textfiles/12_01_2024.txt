Global macros and Domestic markets a mixed bagFollowing volatile global cues, this trading week ended on a mixed note for the Indian indices. The broad-based Nifty 50 index, Nifty Midcap 150 and Nifty Smallcap 250 each ended in green, gaining 0.8%, 0.4% and 1.2% respectively.This was a week of mixed performance for the sectoral indices. Out of the sectoral indices closing in the green; Nifty IT, Nifty Realty and Nifty Oil and Gas were the top performers, gaining 4.8%, 4.3% and 2.6% respectively. On the other hand, Nifty FMCG, Nifty Financial Services and Nifty Bank were the top losers, losing (-) 2.0%, (-) 1.0% and (-) 0.9% respectively.
