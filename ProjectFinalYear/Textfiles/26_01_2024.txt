Global macros negative. Domestic markets a mixed bag With high volatility in the Indian economy owing to the ongoing struggle between FIIs and DIIs, this trading week ended on a mixed note for the Indian indices. The Nifty 50 closed and Nifty Midcap 150 Index closed flat with (-) 0.5% and 0%, during the week. Nifty Smallcap 250 on the other hand closed on a positive note, gaining 1.0%.This was a volatile week for the sectoral indices as well. Out of the sectoral indices closing in the green; Nifty Healthcare Index, Nifty Metal and Nifty Pharma were the top performers, gaining 1.6%, 1.2% and 1.2% respectively. On the other hand, Nifty Media, Nifty Realty and Nifty Private Bank were the top losers, losing (-) 10.8%, (-) 4.5% and (-) 2.3% respectively.
