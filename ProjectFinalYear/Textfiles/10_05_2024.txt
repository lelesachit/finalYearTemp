Bearish Domestic OutlookIndian Equities ended lower this week after a disappointing revenue outlook of a major domestic multinational conglomerate. Adding to it was the uncertainty around the general state elections. This week, Nifty Large cap, Midcap & Small Cap Indexes ended negative. Nifty Small cap 250 Index majorly fell by (4.36)% while Nifty 50 decline by (1.9)%, Nifty Midcap 150 Index registered a loss of (2.56)%.Sectoral indices displayed a mixed performance this week. Nifty FMCG & Nifty Auto were amongst the highest gainers, rising by 1.9% and 1.4% respectively. On the other hand, Nifty PSU Bank, Nifty Media & Nifty Oil & Gas registered negative returns of (6.03) %, (4.25) % and (3.89)% respectively.
