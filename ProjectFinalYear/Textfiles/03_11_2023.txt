Global macros positive. Domestic markets resilient. Tracking positive global cues, most Indian indices closed on a positive note this week with each of the Nifty 50, Nifty Midcap 150, Nifty Small cap 250, gaining 1.0%, 2.1% and 1.9% respectively. This was also a positive week for most of the sectoral indices. Of all the sectors, Nifty Realty, Nifty Media and Nifty Oil and Gas occupied the top position, gaining 10.4%, 4.0% and 3.0% respectively. Only Nifty Auto ended the week in red losing (-) 0.8%, hence settling at the bottom position.
