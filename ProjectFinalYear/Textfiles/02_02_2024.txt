Global macros a mixed bag. Domestic markets mostly positive.Following the interim Budget, this trading week ended mostly on a positive note for the Indian indices. Each of the Nifty 50, Nifty Midcap 150 Index and Nifty Smallcap 250 index ended the week in green, gaining 2.3%, 2.6% and 4.3% respectively. The positive outlook portrays the optimistic market sentiments post the budget presentation.This was a mixed week for the sectoral indices as well. Nifty PSU bank, Nifty Oil and Gas and Nifty Metal were the top performers, gaining 11.5%, 9.1% and 4.6% respectively. On the other hand, only Nifty Media and Nifty FMCG had a flat fall (-) 0.3% and (-) 0.2% respectively.
