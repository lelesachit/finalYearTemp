Indian equity markets logged weekly gains despite weak global cues.Indian equity markets fell after reaching record highs in the first half of the day over concerns of monetary tightening by US Federal Reserve. However, on weekly basis, Indian indices logged gains. The broad-based Nifty 50 Index gained 0.74% during the week, outperforming the Nifty Midcap 150 Index which gained 0.64% and underperforming the Nifty Small Cap 250 Index which grew by 2.21%.Except for Nifty IT, all sectoral indices were positive this week. Nifty Media, Nifty PSU Banks and Nifty Oil and Gas were the top gainers, gaining 8.7%, 8.5% and 4.3% respectively. Nifty IT, Nifty Pharma and Nifty Financial Service Index were at the bottom. The Nifty IT index lost (-) 0.01% while Nifty Pharma and Nifty Financial Services registered negligible gains of 0.01% and 0.1% respectively.
