Benchmark indices end 2% higher this weekThe Domestic broad market benchmarks started the week strongly, but the winning streak was cut short on account of profit booking. The small rally was led by large cap stocks as Nifty 50 gained 2.2% this week. On the other hand, Mid and Small Caps showed muted performance as they inched tad higher: Nifty Midcap 150 gained 0.5% and Nifty Small Cap 250 gained 0.4%.Sectoral indices showed mixed performance. Nifty Oil and Gas, Nifty IT and Nifty Financial Services were the top performing sectors of the week, gaining 3.1%, 2.7% and 1.9% respectively. While Nifty Realty, Nifty Metals and Nifty Media were laggards post losses of (-) 2.4%, (-)1.8% and (-) 2.3% respectively.
