Strong domestic macros boost sentiments.Domestic Equities ended the week on a positive note. The broad-based Nifty 50 Index grew by 2.0% during the week. The mid and small caps outperformed large caps. Nifty Midcap 150 Index and Nifty Small Cap 250 grew by 3.6% and 2.9% respectively.Sectoral indices were a mixed bag with Nifty Media, Nifty Realty and Nifty PSU Bank gaining the most i.e., 5.2%, 4.9% and 3.7% respectively. Whereas Nifty FMCG, Nifty Auto and Nifty Private Bank were at the bottom losing 1.0%, 1.1% and 1.5% respectively.
