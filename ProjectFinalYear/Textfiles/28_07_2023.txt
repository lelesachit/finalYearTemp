Weak trends in US markets drag Indian markets.Indian markets closed almost flat with minor losses as US concerns over rate hikes were reignited this week. The broad-based Nifty 50 Index fell by (-) 0.5% during the week, underperforming Mid and Small caps which showed resistance by registering minor gains. The Nifty Midcap 150 was flat gained 1.5% and Nifty Small cap 250 Index gained 1.2%.Sectoral indices were a mixed bag. Sell off in IT and financial sector dragged Nifty IT and Nifty Bank indices today. During the week, Nifty FMCG, Nifty Bank and Nifty IT fell the most, losing (-) 1.4%, (-) 1.3% and (-) 1.3% respectively. While Nifty Realty, Nifty Pharma and Nifty Media gained the most i.e., 5.0%, 4.8% and 3.4% respectively.
