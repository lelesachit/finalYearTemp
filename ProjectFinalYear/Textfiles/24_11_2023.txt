Markets end the week on a flat note. Most of the broad market indices closed flat this week, with each of the Nifty 50, Nifty Midcap 150, Nifty Small cap 250 closing in green, gaining 0.3%, 0.6% and 0.1% respectively. Indices failed to find pace amidst mixed global cues. Sectoral indices displayed mixed performance this week with most of them displaying positive performance. Out of the indices which closed in green, Nifty Realty, Nifty Oil & Gas, and Nifty Metal occupied the top position, gaining 1.5%, 1.0% and 1.0% respectively. Only Nifty PSU Bank, Nifty FMCG and Nifty IT closed in red losing (-) 1.7%, (-) 0.3% and (-) 0.4% respectively.
