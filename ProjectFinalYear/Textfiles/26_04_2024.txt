A Bullish Sentiment on Global & Domestic MarketIndian equities ended higher due to gains led by banking & metal sectors and also due to await of US first quarter gross domestic product data. Nifty 50 rose with the marginal gain of 1.2%. The Nifty Midcap 150 Index registered a rise of 3.71%, while Nifty Small cap 250 Index rose by 4.30%, during the week, outperforming the large cap index.All Sectoral indices were positive. Nifty PSU Bank, Nifty Oil & Gas and Nifty Realty were the highest gainers with a rise of 6.4%, 5.32% and 4.62% respectively. Nifty Financial Services, Nifty IT, Nifty Private Bank were at the bottom, gaining 0.86%, 0.89% and 0.99% respectively.
