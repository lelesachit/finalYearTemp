Domestic Market Booms as Sectoral Indices Outperform Indian Equities ended positive this week leading the rally after softer than expected US consumer inflation data raised expectations of the US Federal Reserve cutting interest rates. This week, Nifty Large cap, Midcap & Small Cap Indexes ended positive. Nifty Small cap 250 Index & Nifty Midcap 150 Index had outperformed Nifty 50 Index by registering a gain of 4.55% & 4.48% respectively. While Nifty 50 had a gain of 1.9%.Sectoral indices displayed mostly a positive performance this week. Nifty Metal, Nifty Realty & Nifty Consumer Durables were amongst the highest gainers, with gains of 6.5%, 6.6%, & 5.6% respectively. On the other hand, Nifty FMCG has slightly fell by 0.38%, whereas Nifty PSU Bank & Nifty IT had a marginal gain of 0.49% & 1.36% respectively.
