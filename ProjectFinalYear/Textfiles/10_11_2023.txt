Global macros a mixed bag. Domestic markets resilient. Most of the broad market indices closed on a positive note this week, with each of the Nifty 50, Nifty Midcap 150, Nifty Small cap 250 closing in green, gaining 1.0%, 2.6% and 2.4% respectively.Sectoral indices displayed mixed performance this week. Out of the indices which closed in green, Nifty Pharma, Nifty Healthcare Index and Nifty Realty occupied the top position, gaining 4.3%, 4.0% and 2.9% respectively. A few indices closed in red out of which Nifty Media, Nifty Consumer Durables and Nifty PSU Banks were the top losers each losing (-) 1.5%, (-) 0.8% and (-) 0.6% respectively.
