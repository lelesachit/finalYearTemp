A Flat Week for Domestic Equity MarketsIndian Equities experienced a mixed performance but were largely flat for the week. This week, Nifty Large & Midcap Indexes posted gains, while small-cap stocks ended slightly lower. Nifty 50 rose with the marginal gain of 0.2%. The Nifty Midcap 150 Index registered a rise of 0.67% outperforming the large cap index, while Nifty Small cap 250 Index slightly fell by (0.11)%.Sectoral indices also displayed a mixed performance this week. Nifty PSU Bank & Nifty Financial Services were among the highest gainers with a rise of 2.2% and 2.0% respectively. Nifty Media, Nifty IT and Nifty Realty were at the bottom, registering negative returns of (3.1)%, (2.3)% and (0.6)% respectively.
