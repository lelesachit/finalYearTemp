Global macros positive. Domestic markets resilient. Most of the broad market indices closed on a positive note this week, with each of the Nifty 50, Nifty Midcap 150, Nifty Small cap 250 closing in green, gaining 1.6%, 2.5% and 3.4% respectively.Sectoral indices displayed mixed performance this week with most of them displaying positive performance. Out of the indices which closed in green, Nifty IT, Nifty Realty and Nifty Auto occupied the top position, gaining 5.1%, 4.6% and 3.8% respectively. Only Nifty Private Bank, Nifty Bank and Nifty Financial Services closed in red losing (-) 0.5%, (-) 0.5% and (-) 0.1% respectively.
