Global macros a mixed bag. Domestic markets weakThis was a tough week for the Indian equity markets with consecutive weeks of gains in the Nifty 50 and Sensex coming to a halt. Each of the Nifty 50, Nifty Midcap 150, Nifty Small cap 250 closed in the red, losing (-) 0.6%, (-) 1.0% and (-) 0.3% respectively. Though the reason for this sudden fall is not clear yet, rising Covid-19 cases, high profit booking, Red Sea geopolitical tensions, huge number of IPO releases and formation of a small cap bubble over the past few months have been assessed as the probable catalyst for this fall.This was a week of mixed performance for the sectoral indices. Out of the sectoral indices closing in the green; Nifty FMCG, Nifty Consumer Durables and Nifty Oil and Gas were the top performers, gaining 1.6%, 1.4% and 1.4% respectively. Nifty PSU Banks, Nifty Financial Services 25/50 and Nifty Media, on the other hand, closed in the red losing (-) 3.0%, (-) 2.1% and (-) 2,0% respectively.
