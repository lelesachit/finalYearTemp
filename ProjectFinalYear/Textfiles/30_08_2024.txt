Sector Gains & Earnings Drive Mixed OutcomesThis week saw mixed performances across major global stock markets, with varying outcomes influenced by sector-specific gains, economic data, and corporate earnings. Indian stock markets ended the week on a positive note, driven largely by gains in Financial and IT stocks. The Nifty 50 index recorded a gain of 1.7%, outperforming the mid and small cap indices. The Nifty Midcap 150 and Nifty Smallcap 250 indices also rose by 1.3% and 0.9%, respectively.Sector-wise, the Nifty IT index surged by 4.1%, reflecting strong investor confidence in the tech sector. Other notable gainers included the Nifty Pharma and Nifty Realty indices, which rose by 3.0% and 3.5%, respectively. However, the Nifty FMCG and Nifty PSU Bank indices faced declines of 0.5% and 0.45%, respectively. The Nifty Private Bank index posted a modest gain of 0.7%.
