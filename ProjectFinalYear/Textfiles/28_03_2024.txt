Equity markets end the last week of the financial year in greenDomestic equity markets ended the last week of the financial year with decent gains on account of strong domestic flows. Nifty 50 index gained 1.4%. The Nifty Midcap 150 Index and the Nifty Small cap 250 Index outperformed the large cap Index Nifty 50 by gaining 2.1% and 2.0% respectively.Most of the sectoral indices were positive this week. Nifty Realty, Nifty Auto and Nifty Healthcare Index were high performers gaining 4.8%, 3.5% and 3.0% respectively. While Nifty Private Banks, Nifty Media and Nifty IT were at the bottom. Nifty Private Banks was tad positive and showed gains of 0.9%. Whereas Nifty Media and Nifty IT lost (-) 1.7% and (-) 3.1% respectively
